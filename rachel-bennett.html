Rachel Bennett

Under the leadership of Mayor Emily Carson and Economic Development Director Rachel Bennett, Riverview is fostering a thriving economy by supporting small businesses, attracting new industries, and creating job opportunities. Strategic investments and business-friendly policies ensure sustainable growth, making Riverview a hub for innovation and entrepreneurship.

Biography

Rachel Bennett is a visionary leader dedicated to fostering economic growth, supporting local businesses, and creating sustainable development opportunities in Riverview. With over 15 years of experience in economic policy, business development, and urban planning, she has played a crucial role in shaping Riverview’s thriving economy.

Professional Background

Rachel began her career as a business analyst, helping startups and small businesses navigate financial planning and investment strategies. She later transitioned into public service, working with regional economic councils to develop business-friendly policies, attract investors, and create job opportunities. Her expertise in economic strategy and urban development has positioned Riverview as a competitive, business-friendly city.

Achievements & Contributions

  • Led initiatives that increased small business growth by 30% over the past five years.
  • Developed incentive programs that attracted major companies and industries to Riverview.
  • Spearheaded revitalization projects that boosted local tourism and commerce.
  • Championed sustainable economic policies to balance growth with environmental responsibility.
  • Strengthened partnerships between government, private investors, and community organizations.

Rachel Bennett’s dedication and strategic approach continue to drive Riverview’s economic success, making it a dynamic place for businesses and residents alike.
