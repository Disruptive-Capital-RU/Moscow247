Key Services Offered

Keeping Riverview clean & green

Household Trash Collection

  • Weekly curbside pickup for residential neighborhoods.
  • Scheduled collection days vary by district.
  • Large item disposal available upon request.

Recycling Program

  • Collection of paper, plastic, glass, and metal.
  • Free recycling bins provided to residents.
  • Drop-off recycling centers for additional waste disposal.

Yard Waste & Composting

  • Organic waste pickup available for leaves, grass clippings, and branches.
  • Community composting initiatives to reduce organic waste impact.
