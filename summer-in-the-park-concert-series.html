Summer in the Park Concert Series



Riverview Park



July 9, 2025
-
July 12, 2025



Free
Register for the event

Spend your summer evenings enjoying live music under the stars at Riverview’s Summer in the Park Concert Series. This annual event brings together local and national artists for a season of great music, delicious food, and a lively community atmosphere.

Event Highlights:

  • Live performances featuring rock, jazz, country, and pop artists
  • Food trucks and local vendors offering great eats
  • Family-friendly lawn seating – bring blankets and chairs
  • Special themed nights and guest performers

Gather your friends and family, relax by the river, and soak in the sounds of summer. Arrive early to grab a good spot, and enjoy a perfect Friday night out!
